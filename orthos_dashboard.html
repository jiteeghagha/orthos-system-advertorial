Why these three?
☀️
D₃ + K₂ — The Foundation
D₃ stimulates calcium absorption; K₂ ensures it goes into bone rather than soft tissue. Together they build the skeletal density your posture depends on. Critical for adults over 40 — most are severely deficient.
🌙
Magnesium Glycinate — The Relaxer
Chronically tight postural muscles pull the spine out of alignment. Magnesium acts as a natural muscle relaxant and helps the nervous system downregulate the chronic tension patterns that cause poor posture.
🦴
Collagen Peptides — The Rebuild
Ligaments, tendons, and intervertebral discs are made largely of collagen. As we age, production drops. Supplementing provides the raw material to rebuild the connective tissue that holds spinal alignment.
