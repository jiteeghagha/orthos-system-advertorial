Why these three?
☀️
D₃ + K₂ — The Foundation
D₃ stimulates calcium absorption; K₂ ensures it goes into bone. Together they build the skeletal density your posture depends on. Most adults over 40 are severely deficient.
🌙
Magnesium Glycinate — The Relaxer
Chronically tight postural muscles pull the spine out of alignment. Magnesium acts as a natural muscle relaxant and helps the nervous system downregulate the tension patterns that cause poor posture.
🦴
Collagen Peptides — The Rebuild
Ligaments, tendons, and intervertebral discs are made largely of collagen. Supplementing provides the raw material to rebuild the connective tissue that holds spinal alignment.
